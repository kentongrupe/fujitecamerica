Fujitec GS8000 NX Escalator Series

The Fujitec GS8000 NX is a sophisticated and ergonomically designed escalator series engineered for safety and performance. There are three available Types with corresponding Models that offer varying safety, aesthetic, performance and capacity solutions.

Type S

“Type S” or SLIM: Indoor Use Only
MODEL:
  • S600 = 24” Riser
  • S800 = 32” Riser
  • S1000 = 40” Riser

DESCRIPTION: Transparent, tempered glass interior panel construction reveals a clean and refined look.

Type F

“Type F” or FRAME: Indoor & Outdoor Use
MODEL:
  • F600 = 24” Riser
  • F800 = 32” Riser
  • F1000 = 40” Riser

DESCRIPTION: Transparent, tempered glass interior panel construction. The Type F incorporates an external stainless steel enclosure underneath the handrail which is attached to the exterior of the frame. This protects internal components from exposure to external conditions such as rain, dust, sand, snow, ice, etc. This model type can incorporate under-rail lighting for safety and aesthetics as an option.

Type P

“Type P” or PANEL: Indoor & Outdoor Use
MODEL:
  • P600 = 24” Riser
  • P800 = 32” Riser
  • P1000 = 40” Riser

DESCRIPTION: Hairline-finished, stainless steel interior panel construction ideally suited for public transit and commercial facility applications. The Type P also incorporates an external stainless steel enclosure underneath the handrail to protect internal components from external -conditions which can also support optional under-rail lighting for safety and aesthetics.
