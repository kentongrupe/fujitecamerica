EZ-Shuttle

Fujitec’s EZShuttle is a Destination Floor Guidance System (DFGS) that reduces the expected number of stops, decreasing travel time during up-peak periods. The EZShuttle works in tandem with the Fujitec Viridian Controller and is best utilized for high-rise applications exceeding 40+ stories with excessive traffic and high occupancy.

Features and Benefits

  1. Enhance building safety
  2. Personal concierge capabilities
  3. Card reader security application
  4. Increase traffic efficiency
  5. Better traffic organization in lobbies
  6. Reduce destination travel time

How it works

  1. Passenger registers desired floor using the keypad on the EZ-Shuttle fixture
  2. Elevator location/assignment is presented on the EZ-Shuttle display.
  3. Passenger moves to designated elevator and waits for arrival.
  4. While boarding, destinations are displayed on the floor indicator at the car’s entrance column.

*Note: If a passenger inadvertently boards the wrong elevator, they must exit the car at the next available stop and re-register their destination. The EZShuttle eliminates buttons and the ability to change destinations within the car.

Configuration/Applications

  1. All Floor: EZShuttle fixture units at each floor
  2. Lobby: EZShuttle fixture unit at ground-level (conventional up/down buttons and hall lanterns on remaining floors)
  3. Combination: Apply a number of the above scenarios to meet traffic needs.
