MONITORING

webEMIS

Fujitec developed webEMIS to improve building safety and elevator efficiency. Real-time operations are monitored and adjusted from one or more locations, providing valuable traffic analysis in an effort to improve building efficiency during up-peak and down-peak traffic times.

Benefits & Features

  • 24/7 Operational status monitoring
  • Localized elevator control
  • Communicates system issues
  • Dispatch hall calls/car calls & lock-outs
  • Allow/deny access for security support
  • Specialized destination requests
  • Custom traffic performance reports

How it Works

webEMIS exchanges real-time data with an elevator’s control system to determine operational status and floor location. Status is controlled by the client and programmed to satisfy ‘normal group operation’ or ‘out of service’ scenarios. webEMIS also handles a variety of specialized requests such as ‘VIP dispatch’ or ‘special assistance’ operations.

VIP Dispatch

Assign a unit to service specific floors/locations within a building

Special Assistance

Assign a unit to accommodate special needs tenants by adjusting door operating speeds and increased landing durations for boarding

Installation

Fujitec’s webEMIS is available on new or existing Fujitec Millennium and Viridian equipment. Software communicates through an independent/secure server (supplied by Fujitec) via the building’s network, and includes a dedicated client computer (supplied by Fujitec) for monitoring and operation.

Fujitec IONFUL® Improve the Air You Breathe

As we continue to search for ways to improve Fujitec products, safety is at the forefront of our goal which includes passenger health. Cab interiors can be a safe haven for airborne viruses and allergens, Fujitec’s Ionful® incorporates technology from the Sharp Corporation to address these issues.

Clean Air Cab with IONFUL®

Specifically designed for cab interiors, IONFUL® transports ions via generator through the ventilation system where they attach, surround and break down potentially harmful particles, releasing purified water vapor as the byproduct.

IONFUL® is energy efficient and operates when movement is detected inside the cab.

IMPROVE ENERGY SAVINGS: Add a Re-Generative Drive

Adding a “Re-Generative Drive” to your system, acts as a line-power inverter by capturing energy produced by the elevator during over-hauling load conditions and recycling it back into the building’s power grid. Fujitec offers this option to further reduce energy consumption.
