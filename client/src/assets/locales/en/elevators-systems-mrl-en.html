Energy Saving Solutions

Fujitec’s Viridian MRL incorporates a permanent magnet gearless (PMGL) motor that minimizes energy consumption when compared to traditional systems.

Improve Energy Savings: Add a Re-Gen Drive

Adding a “Re-Gen Drive” to your system, acts as a line-power inverter by capturing energy produced by the elevator during over-hauling load conditions and recycling it back into the building’s power grid. Fujitec offers this option to further reduce energy consumption.
